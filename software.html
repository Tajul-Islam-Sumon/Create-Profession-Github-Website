Software

MoBaS

A software to find disease associated subnetworks in protein-protein interaction (PPI) networks.

PoCoS

A tool for prioritizing locus pairs for testing epistasis and risk assessment.

CoPPNet

A software to identify modules in the weighted network.

CophosK

A tool to leverage mass spectrometry phosphoproteomics data to predict kinase-substrate interactions

PhosphoEnrichment

A tool to fascilitate the kinase-substrate enrichment analysis and pathway enrichment analysis at the site and protein level.
